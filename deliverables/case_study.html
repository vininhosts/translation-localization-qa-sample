Project Summary

This sample translates and localizes product copy for TaskPilot AI, a fictional productivity assistant for meeting summaries and action items. The goal is to keep the Portuguese clear, natural, concise, and suitable for software UI and app-store use.

Before and After Sample

English Source

Stay focused without losing track of the details. TaskPilot AI captures your notes, highlights decisions, and suggests action items so your team can move faster after every meeting.

Review the suggested action items before sharing them. You stay in control of what gets sent.

Brazilian Portuguese Translation

Mantenha o foco sem perder os detalhes importantes. O TaskPilot AI captura suas anotações, destaca decisões e sugere itens de ação para que sua equipe avance com mais rapidez depois de cada reunião.

Revise os itens de ação sugeridos antes de compartilhá-los. Você mantém o controle sobre o que será enviado.

Quality Checks

  • Meaning preserved from source to target.
  • Brazilian Portuguese reads naturally, not literally translated.
  • Product name remains unchanged.
  • UI strings are concise enough for buttons and menus.
  • Terminology is consistent across app-store copy, onboarding, UI, and help center text.
  • Safety/help-center warning remains clear and direct.
