# Source Text: TaskPilot AI

TaskPilot AI is a lightweight productivity assistant for small teams. It helps users organize tasks, summarize meeting notes, and turn long conversations into clear next steps.

## App Store Description

Stay focused without losing track of the details. TaskPilot AI captures your notes, highlights decisions, and suggests action items so your team can move faster after every meeting.

Use TaskPilot AI to:

- summarize meeting transcripts
- extract action items and owners
- organize project notes by topic
- rewrite messy notes into clear updates
- prepare follow-up messages for clients and teammates

TaskPilot AI is designed for busy founders, consultants, freelancers, and remote teams that need practical summaries instead of more noise.

## Help Center Excerpt

TaskPilot AI does not replace your judgment. Always review generated summaries and action items before sending them to clients or teammates. For best results, include speaker names, dates, and enough context for the assistant to understand the meeting.

If your transcript contains sensitive information, remove confidential details before uploading it. You can delete generated summaries from your workspace at any time.
